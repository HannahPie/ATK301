MoMA: On Demand
Spring 2017

CLIENT
Illinois State University

CONCEPT
This is a brochure advertising the Museum of Modern Art's new online class program, "MoMA: On Demand". The information is organized into a promotional piece that would be sent out to prospective students. A spread is dedicated to each class, which includes information about the courses and instructors. The color scheme is eye-catching, yet subdued to appeal to students while maintaining a professional feel. Blue and orange are used as complements that work together throughout the piece. The use of transparencies suggests the depth and possibilities of the internet, while connecting pages, making the spreads work together as one
cohesive unit.
