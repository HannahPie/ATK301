Labyrinth Made Goods
Spring 2020

CLIENT
Young Women's Christian Association (YWCA)

CONCEPT
Labyrinth Made Goods is a non-for-profit organization that is founded under YWCA Labyrinth Outreach Services to Women. This sector of YWCA provides services to help formerly incarcerated women reintegrate into society. The goal of Labyrinth Made Goods is to establish a place of work for these women to gain professional experience and to help their reintegration process. Their first product is a hand-poured candle.

The logo uses the typeface "Arkhip", a sans serif typeface that is clean and polished and modified to portray a unique, handmade flair. Four seasons of candles, along with a specialty Serenity candle, were designed. Each season is designated its own color, each radiating a sense of warmth and brightness that illustrates the kindness which Labyrinth Made Goods is founded upon. Along with a color, each season posesses a unique illustration drawn with detail in a botanical style, highlighting the handmade process of creating the candles. Overall, each candle exhudes a sense of modern professionalism and classic sophistication that gives Labyrinth Made Goods a professional groundwork to work upon while also capturing their warm, accepting personality as a business.
