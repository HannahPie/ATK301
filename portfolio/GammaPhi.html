Gamma Phi Circus
Spring 2020

CLIENT
University Marketing and Communications,
Illinois State University's Gamma Phi Circus

CONCEPT
This shirt was created for the alumni of Gamma Phi Circus at Illinois State University. Because this shirt would be a keepsake for alumni of the group, I took into account the feeling of nostalgia. Nostalgia looks to the past and creates beautiful images in the mind based on memory and feeling. I looked to retro circus design and Victorian type for inspiration to create a typographic image. The fluidity of thoughts and memory are illustrated through the flowing lines and shapes throughout the piece. The baseline of type curves upward to represent this fluidity, and also to depict the positivity of nostalgic thoughts and how they can propel us to create a better future. The swirls and cascading circles call to mind the motion of circus routines that combine elements of dance and gymnastics to create a visually dynamic performance. A navy blue T-shirt serves to remind the viewer of looking at the night sky in fond rememberence of the past, and seeing memories play out in the white glowing stars above.
